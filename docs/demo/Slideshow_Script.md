# Slideshow Script

Working document for the Moonrise Watcher Assistant demo slideshow (Microsoft Clipchamp).

Each slide has a **description** of what is shown, followed by one or more **narration options**.
Each narration option has its own duration and a note explaining how it differs from the others.

---

## Slide 1 — App Icon

**Description:** The app icon displayed on its own. Opening shot before the voice begins.

**Duration:** ~2 sec

*(no narration)*

---

## Slide 2 — Welcome Screen

**Description:** The app's welcome screen — the first thing a new user sees when they open the app.

### Narration A — First Take
**Duration:** ~14 sec
> "This is Moonrise Watcher — an app that tells you which nights are worth stepping outside to catch
> a moonrise. It combines moon phase data, timing, and your local weather forecast to give you a
> simple answer: good night, or not worth it."

### Narration B — Shortened Version
**Instructions** - make Narration A shorter
**Duration:** ~7 sec
> "Moonrise Watcher tells you which nights are great for watching the moonrise, based on moon phase,
> timing, and local weather."

### Narration C — With Transition
**Instructions** - Start with Narration B, add transition to Slide 3
**Duration:** ~10 sec
> "Moonrise Watcher tells you which nights are great for watching the moonrise, based on moon phase,
> timing, and local weather. When you first open the app, it asks you to add your location."

**Final Narration:** Narration C

## Slide 3 — Setup first location

**Description:** Setting up the first location in `Wilton, New Hampshire` and using label `My Home`
for the location.

### Narration A — First Take
**Duration:** ~10 sec
> "The first time you open the app, it asks you to add a location. You type in your city, give it a
> label, and hit Save. That's all the setup there is."

### Narration B — Specific to the Demo
**Instructions** - Reference the location shown on screen
**Duration:** ~8 sec
> "Here we're adding Wilton, New Hampshire, labeled My Home. The app will use this to calculate
> moonrise times and pull the local weather forecast."

### Narration C — Combined
**Instructions** - Let us combine Narration A (instructions for first time launch) with Narration B
**Duration:** ~11 sec
> " You type in your city and give it
> a label — here we're adding Wilton, New Hampshire, labeled My Home. The app uses this to calculate
> moonrise times and pull the local weather forecast."

**Final Narration:** Narration C
